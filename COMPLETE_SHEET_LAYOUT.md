## Summary of All Sections

| Section | Purpose | Key Info | Colors |
|---------|---------|----------|--------|
| **1. Key Metrics** | Quick overview | Total cases, counts of new/lost | Blue header, green/red rows |
| **2. Customer Tracking** | Who is new/lost | Actual customer names by month | Purple header, green/red sections |
| **3. Pivot Table** | Detailed breakdown | All customers, products, monthly data | Red header, colorful rows |

---

## Data Flow

```
Upload Data → Google Apps Script Processes → Creates 3 Sections:

1. Calculates monthly totals & counts
2. Identifies specific new/lost customer names  
3. Groups customers → sub-vendors → products

Result: Beautiful, comprehensive monthly report!
```

---

## Key Advantages

### 📊 For Quick Analysis:
- **Section 1**: See totals at a glance
- **Section 2**: Identify specific customers to follow up with
- **Section 3**: Drill down into details

### 🎯 For Action Items:
- **New customers** (Section 2): Welcome them, ensure smooth onboarding
- **Lost customers** (Section 2): Immediate outreach opportunity
- **Product trends** (Section 3): Identify what's selling where

### 📈 For Reporting:
- **Export Section 1**: Executive summary
- **Export Section 2**: Sales team accountability
- **Export Section 3**: Operations planning

## Updates Automatically

Every upload through your SalesTracker app rebuilds all three sections with the latest data!
